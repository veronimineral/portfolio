About me

My name is Weronika Schabowicz, currently living in Cracow, Poland, studying Applied Computer Science at AGH University of Science and Technology. My main field of interest is working as frontend developer, creating modern websites using HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and more. I'm also instrested in creating graphics using Inkscape, Adobe Photoshop or Figma.
In my free time I'm all about exploring secrets of the astronomy, Japanese culture and best cuisines of the world.
